# Swiggy-Sales-Analysis


🧠 📌 KPIs (Key Metrics)
👉 These are the main business indicators you analyzed:
Total Sales → Overall revenue generated
Total Orders → Total number of orders placed
Average Order Value (AOV) → Revenue per order
Average Rating → Customer satisfaction level
Rating Count → Total number of ratings given

📈 📊 Charts / Analysis Performed
🔹 Time-Based Analysis
Monthly Sales Trends
Weekly Sales Trends
Daily Sales Trends
Quarterly Sales Performance

👉 Helps understand growth, seasonality & fluctuations

🔹 Location-Based Analysis
Total Sales by State
Top 5 Cities by Revenue

👉 Helps identify high-performing regions


🔹 Product-Based Analysis
Total Sales by Food Type

👉 Helps understand customer preferences


💡 🔥 Business Insights :-

Sales show clear weekly and monthly fluctuations, indicating changing customer demand
Certain weeks/months act as peak periods, possibly due to offers or weekends
A few cities contribute majority of revenue, showing strong regional demand
Some food categories generate higher sales, indicating popular choices
Average rating and rating count help measure customer satisfaction and engagement


🏁 🎯 Final Conclusion

The analysis provides insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and regional demand patterns. These findings can help optimize marketing strategies, improve customer experience, and focus on high-performing locations and products.
